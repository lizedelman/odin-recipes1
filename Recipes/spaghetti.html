Spaghetti

It's super easy, isn't that cool

This is a paragraph that talks about how easy spaghetti is to make .

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti noodles
  • 1 cloves garlic
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp of basil, oregano, parsley
  • 1 tbsp salt

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti, drain water when done
  2. Add sauce, serve.
  3. Enjoy the ziti with a lot of red wine
